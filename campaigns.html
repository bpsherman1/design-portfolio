Cancer Won't Wait, Will You?

2024 — Saatchi & Saatchi Wellness (Internship, Art Director)

Professional campaign developed during an internship at Saatchi & Saatchi Wellness, where I served as art director. The campaign raises awareness about early cancer screening, spanning posters, brand guidelines, subway advertising, digital ads for Instagram and Twitter, physical info cards, and a full bus wrap.
