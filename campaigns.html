Cancer Won't Wait, Will You?

2024 — Saatchi & Saatchi Wellness (Internship, Art Director)

Professional campaign developed during an internship at Saatchi & Saatchi Wellness, where I served as art director. The campaign raises awareness about early cancer screening, spanning posters, brand guidelines, subway advertising, digital ads for Instagram and Twitter, physical info cards, and a full bus wrap.

Advertisements

For this project, I designed a variety of ads, particularly for either print or digital. This first ad was designed as a NYC subway sign, which could appear in a NYC subway.

Digital Ads

Designed for digital media, I created two layouts for both Instagram and Twitter.

Physical Ad

Lastly, we wanted to design a physical advertisement, so we created an info card that can be handed out.

Stop Climate Change, Prevent Wildfires

2024 — UW-Madison

A multi-format awareness campaign connecting climate change to wildfire prevention. Includes poster design, editorial layouts, phone mockups for digital distribution, and gallery/event mockups showing environmental installation.

Protect Our Parks

2024 — UW-Madison

Instagram-native social media campaign advocating for the protection of Yellowstone and Grand Teton National Parks. Designed as carousel posts and stories, optimized for mobile engagement with bold typography and park photography.
